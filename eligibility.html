Eligibility Criteria

Academic Qualification

This program is open to students who meet one of the following academic criteria:

  • Currently pursuing studies in Class IX.
  • Currently pursuing studies in Class X.
  • Currently pursuing studies in Class XI.
  • Currently pursuing studies in Class XII.

Other Requirements

Students currently pursuing their studies in Class XI or Class XII must have opted for the Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics (PCM) subject combination to be eligible for this program.
